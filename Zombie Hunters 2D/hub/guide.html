How To Play

Controls:

  • Use W, A, S, and D to move around the map
  • Left Click to shoot, but be mindful of your ammo
  • Use E to refill your ammo when standing on the ammo crates
  • Use Q to cycle through your weapons
  • Use SPACE to switch to a knife
  • Use ESC to pause

Gameplay:

  • Kill zombies or die trying
  • After every round 1 more zombie spawns, quickly making a swarm
  • At round 5 you meet the raged & robust zombies
  • Use your quick thinking to reserve ammo without dying
  • You can use the gold you collect to buy powerups to help you survive
  • Have Fun (And don't die)

  • Triggered by pressing 3
